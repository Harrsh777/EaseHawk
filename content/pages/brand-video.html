Before You Write

Asked often. Answered straight.

How do you use AI in creative work?
In the pipeline, never on the final frame: AI-assisted research, edit iteration and localisation compress timelines, then human craft signs everything that ships. It's how four market adaptations run in parallel without four parallel budgets.
Can you handle multi-market, multi-language campaigns?
It's a specialty: four APAC markets for one food brand in a single year, and Arabic-market work across the GCC. Cultural adaptation is treated as strategy, not translation.
Retainer or project — which should we pick?
Steady launch calendar: retainer (our Tigo engagement is three years and counting). One flagship film or a single campaign: project. Both are priced after one scoping call.
What's a realistic turnaround?
Retained clients get announcement-speed delivery — assets cleared against three brand systems have shipped inside partnership launch windows. One-off projects are scoped honestly upfront; we'd rather lose a rush job than miss a date.
