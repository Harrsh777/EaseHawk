Cookie Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026 · EaseHawk Technologies Private Limited

This policy explains the current, honest state of cookies and similar technologies on easehawk.com.

1. Cookies we set ourselves

None. The Site's own code does not set cookies and does not use local storage to track you. Our forms open your email application rather than storing data in your browser, and the on-site assistant runs from a script inside the page.

2. Third-party technologies

  • Google Fonts & cdnjs: load typefaces and one code library; requests to these services expose standard technical data (such as your IP address) to them.
  • Embedded video (YouTube/Vimeo): only if you press play on our Video Portfolio page — the player is loaded at that moment and the platform may set its own cookies under its own policy.
  • Calendly: only if you choose to open our booking page, on Calendly's own site.

3. What's coming (and our promise)

We intend to add privacy-respecting analytics to understand which pages help visitors most. Before any analytics or advertising technology that uses cookies goes live, this policy will be updated and, where required by law, a consent banner will be introduced. Until then: no banner, because there's nothing to consent to.

4. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings at any time; blocking third-party cookies will not break this Site.

info@easehawk.com — one business day, as always.
