Before You Write

Asked often. Answered straight.

Our data is a mess — where do we even start?
Where everyone starts: one decision that matters, traced back to the data it needs. We build that path first — model, pipeline, dashboard — then every next report ships faster than the last because the foundations are reusable.
Why Power BI?
Because it's where enterprise reporting lives — and we've run it at semiconductor-fab scale for a full year, embedded. That said, the modelling discipline underneath matters more than the tool; good foundations outlive any BI front-end.
Embedded specialist or project delivery — which fits?
If you need a reporting estate built, project delivery. If you need the capability inside your team, embedded — our UTAC engagement ran a year with knowledge transfer throughout, so the capability stayed when we left.
What does 'AI-ready' actually mean?
Modelled, governed, documented data that a language model can query without hallucinating your numbers. It's the difference between AI on top of chaos and AI on top of truth — and it's cheaper to build in now than to retrofit later.
